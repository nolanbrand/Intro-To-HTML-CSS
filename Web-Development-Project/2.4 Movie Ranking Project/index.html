The Best Movies According to Nolan

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. Gladiator

Though not historically accurate, this movie has lots of action and drama. Very exciting and well worth a watch!

2. Saving Private Ryan

Includes one of the most realistic dipictions of the beach landings on Omaha beach during WWII. It depicts a platoon of men going into enemy lines to rescue a man whose brothers were all killed in action.

3. Clockwork Orange

In a dystopian London, a group of troubled young men are in a gang that terrorize the people. The leader, Alex is arrested and put through an experimental treatment program which alters his psyche to force him to behave like an upstanding citizen.
